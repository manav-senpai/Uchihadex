Study Techniques

🧠 Why Smart Study Matters

Studying smarter, not harder, helps retain more in less time. It builds deeper understanding, increases productivity, and reduces burnout.

📝 Techniques You Can Use

  • Pomodoro Method: 25 mins focused study + 5 min break. Repeat 4x, then take a long break.
  • Active Recall: Instead of rereading, test yourself after every topic.
  • Spaced Repetition: Review material at increasing intervals over time.
  • Feynman Technique: Teach the topic as if explaining it to a beginner.
  • Mind Mapping: Visualize connections between concepts.

🧩 Focus Boosters

  • Keep your study space clean and organized.
  • Use ambient or instrumental music (like lofi beats).
  • Use website blockers during study time.
  • Sleep well before big study sessions.

📖 Study Resources

Use these to deepen your learning:

  • Online platforms: Khan Academy, Coursera, YouTube Edu
  • Flashcards: Anki, Quizlet
  • Study with friends or group discussions

🎯 Goal-Oriented Planning

Always start with clear, realistic goals. Write down what you want to achieve in each session. Track progress weekly.
