Diet & Nutrition

🥦 What is Nutrition?

Nutrition is the science of food and how it affects our health and performance. It includes everything from macronutrients (like carbs, proteins, and fats) to micronutrients (vitamins and minerals).

📊 Macronutrients Breakdown

Carbohydrates: Main source of energy. Found in rice, bread, oats, fruits.

Proteins: Build and repair muscle. Found in eggs, chicken, legumes, dairy.

Fats: Essential for hormone production and brain function. Found in nuts, seeds, fish.

🍌 Micronutrients Essentials

Micronutrients include vitamins (A, B, C, D, E, K) and minerals like iron, calcium, magnesium, and zinc. These regulate important functions like immunity, bone strength, and energy metabolism.

📅 Daily Healthy Habits

  • Drink at least 2-3 liters of water.
  • Eat 3 balanced meals a day with 2 small snacks.
  • Reduce sugar and processed food intake.
  • Prioritize whole foods and colorful fruits/veggies.
  • Practice mindful eating — chew slowly, eat without screens.

📋 Sample Ninja Meal Plan

Breakfast: Oatmeal with banana and peanut butter + green tea.

Lunch: Grilled chicken or tofu + brown rice + stir-fried veggies.

Dinner: Mixed vegetable soup + multigrain toast + fruit.

Snacks: Nuts, yogurt, or boiled eggs.

🔔 Quick Note:

Stay hydrated and take breaks!
