Physical Health

💪 What is Physical Health?

Physical health is about maintaining a fit and active body. It includes regular exercise, proper nutrition, sleep, and avoiding harmful habits. A healthy body fuels a sharp and focused mind.

⚙️ Benefits of Staying Active

  • Improves heart health and stamina
  • Boosts mood and energy levels
  • Enhances focus and memory
  • Regulates body weight and muscle tone
  • Reduces risk of chronic illnesses

🏋️‍♂️ Daily Exercise Ideas

  • Stretch or do yoga for 10 minutes in the morning
  • Go for a brisk 30-minute walk or jog
  • Do basic home workouts — push-ups, squats, planks
  • Dance, cycle, or play outdoor games for fun movement

😴 Rest and Recovery

Sleep is just as important as movement. Aim for 7–9 hours of restful sleep. Take rest days between intense workouts. Let your muscles and mind recharge.

🚫 Habits to Avoid

  • Skipping meals or eating too late at night
  • Sitting for long periods without breaks
  • Using gadgets right before sleep
  • Overtraining without rest days

🔔 Quick Note:

Motion creates emotion — stay active, stay sharp.
