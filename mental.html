Mental Health

💬 What is Mental Health?

Mental health refers to emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It's important at every stage of life, from childhood to adulthood.

📉 Common Stress Triggers

  • Academic or work pressure
  • Social expectations and comparisons
  • Loneliness or isolation
  • Lack of physical activity or sleep
  • Unhealthy lifestyle habits

🧘‍♂️ Ways to Boost Mental Health

  • Practice mindfulness or meditation daily
  • Get enough sleep and eat well
  • Talk to someone you trust
  • Take regular breaks from screens and social media
  • Exercise regularly — even a short walk helps!

💡 When to Seek Help

If you're feeling overwhelmed, anxious, or sad for extended periods — it's okay to ask for help. Reach out to counselors, helplines, or trusted mentors. You're not alone, and support is always available.

📖 Self-Care Practices for Students

  • Write a daily journal to clear your mind
  • Listen to calming music or ambient sounds
  • Create a relaxing study-break ritual
  • Limit caffeine during high-stress weeks
  • Declutter your digital and physical space

Take a deep breath — you're doing your best.
